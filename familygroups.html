Church is Family

Family Groups

You weren’t meant to go through life alone. God Himself exists in community—Father, Son and Holy Spirit—and we’re all sons and daughters of our Heavenly Father. You were created to laugh, work, shed tears and share the joy of salvation with a family.

Feel at home and grow with God in an intimate setting, apart from Sunday worship.

The Journey Church is made of more than a dozen “family groups” spanning multiple neighborhoods and four college campuses. Visit a family group gathering and discover the blessing of sharing life with other Christians. Here, you’ll connect with 10-15 people of similar age who meet weekly to study God’s word, pray, serve and journey through life together.
