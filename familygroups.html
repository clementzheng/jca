Family Groups

Community for the moments that matter

Experience the depths of God’s love through honest, Biblical friendships. We call our Bible study small groups “Family Group” because joining a church means joining a family.

You weren’t meant to go through life alone. God longs to see us loving one another with brotherly affection: A new commandment I give to you, that you love one another: just as I have loved you, you also are to love one another. John 13:34

It’s in this weekly, intimate space where we get to ask honest questions, form lifelong friendships, engage daily life with God’s word, and learn what it means to serve others.
