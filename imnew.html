We're so glad
you're here

Journey Church is a community all about God and all about people.

Jesus calls us to be a family that extends grace, mercy and forgiveness to everyone and loves others as Jesus has loved us. We want to see people saved, set free, transformed and then empowered to serve others. No matter what you’ve been through or where you are in your relationship with Jesus, you’re welcome here.

College students, professionals, and families who call Journey their home are passionate about investing in communities and believe that Jesus can transform anyone’s life. We strive to follow Him, raise up leaders, and plant churches in Georgia, in the U.S.A., and all around the world because Jesus gave us this glorious command and promise:

Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age. (Matthew 28:19–20)

We long to see the multiplication, maturation, and mobilization of passionate followers of Jesus Christ in a church planting movement. FInd out more about our emphasis on missional living here.

Parents, need childcare?

Submit this form if you’d like your child to participate in “Little Travelers” Sunday School, or need additional childcare, and we’ll contact you within 24 hours. During the 1:30 PM Sunday service at Eastside, we hold “Little Travelers” Sunday School for children in grades kindergarten and up. We’d love for your children to participate. If you have children younger than kindergarten age, please let us know via the above form so we can make the most suitable arrangements.

Before your Sunday visit

Please fill out this form to let us know the names and ages of your children, as well as any allergies or other important notes.

On Sunday

Let any greeter on the Welcoming Team know that you would like your children to participate in Little Travelers. The greeter will then escort your family to the Sunday School room.

Go deeper with a small group

To us, church is family. Visit a small, “family” group gathering and discover the joy of sharing life with other brothers and sisters. Here, you’ll connect with 10-15 people who gather weekly to study God’s word, pray, serve, laugh, cry and do life together. You weren’t meant to journey through life alone.
