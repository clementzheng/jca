Get to know us

Who We Are

The Journey Church of Atlanta is a community all about God and all about people. Jesus calls us to be a family that extends grace, mercy and forgiveness to everyone, and to love others as Jesus has loved us. Striving to do things with excellence and love, we want to see people saved, set free, discipled and empowered to love and serve others. This is a place where alongside peers you can work out your personal faith no matter what you’ve been through or where you are in your relationship with Jesus.

We’re specifically passionate about inspiring college students because we believe that if you can change college students you can change the world. So in addition to young professionals and families, we go to the collegiates and hold services and small group (Bible studies) on four different campuses around Atlanta.

Visit a Family Group

Life is meant to be lived in community. We were made to live enjoy life in community (Father, Son and Holy Spirit). You were created to laugh, work, play and to share the joy of salvation with everyone.

A family group is a group of 10–15 people who gather weekly to study God’s word, pray, share our struggles and blessings and build Biblical friendships. Groups are usually formed by similar life stage. JCA offers you a chance to connect with others and grow with God through small, family setting.

We encourage you to join a family group, and experience life with other people.
