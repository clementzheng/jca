Disciples of
all nations

Jesus called His believers to a great and glorious mission:
Go and make disciples of all nations. Matthew 28:19

God has called us to missions both locally, in our neighborhoods and in our country, and globally. Therefore, we highly value our missional partners and see them as essential components of the church.

We’ve teamed up with various ministries and nonprofit organizations in the Atlanta area, and we hope to continue to partner with them to minister to the underserved. Journey is also committed to sending teams to locations all around the world on short-term (weeks to months) and long-term (one year or more) trips.

Local Partners

But seek the welfare of the city where I have sent you into exile, and pray to the Lord on its behalf, for in its welfare you will find your welfare. Jeremiah 29:7 ESV

City of Refuge

City of Refuge exists to bring light, hope, and transformation to individuals and families of Atlanta. We partner with people in crisis to help break the cycle of poverty through services in health & wellness, housing, human trafficking intervention, vocational training and youth development.


City Hope Community

City Hope Community’s mission is to mobilize and unite individuals and churches in taking up the care and cause of the socially vulnerable. We long to see communities where members can flourish and extend God’s love as salt and light to the world.


Journey Community Church (Durham, NC)

Originally part of the Journey Church of Atlanta’s planting team, Dave was sent out to plant a church in the Research Triangle area of North Carolina. Journey Community Church opened its doors in the fall of 2011. They minister on the campuses of Duke, UNC, and NC State and among many families in the area.
