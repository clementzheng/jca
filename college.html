Family Groups

Experience the depths of God’s love through honest, Biblical friendships. We call our Bible study small groups “Family Group” because joining a church means joining a family.

We really get it. We went through it. The highs. The lows. The fear and the excitement. We've learned that it's so much better together, and we'd love to walk with you through it all.

Family Groups are safe spaces to ask honest questions, make mistakes, and experience divine love during some of the most tumultuous times in life. Think of it as a cozy living room full of friends to discuss faith, ask questions about God’s word, and share the struggles and victories of everyday life.
