We’re so glad
you’re here

Journey Church is a community
all about God and all about people.

Jesus calls us to be a family that extends grace, mercy and forgiveness to everyone, and to love others as Jesus has loved us. Striving to serve with love and excellence, we want to see people saved, set free, transformed and empowered to serve others.

This is a community where, alongside peers, you can work out your personal faith no matter what you’ve been through or where you are in your relationship with Jesus.

Not only are we go-getters and young families, but we are also passionate about building relationships with college students.

If you change a college student’s life, you change the world.

So in addition to our Eastside location, we go directly to the campuses. We hold Sunday service in Midtown and small groups at Georgia State, Georgia Tech, Emory and Oxford College.

Join us on Sundays

At Journey, you’ll find people of diverse backgrounds who are excited to gather together. Our Sunday celebration services are packed with Spirit-filled worship, impactful and expositional Bible teaching, and an opportunity to connect with people who are excited about God and the world we live in.
