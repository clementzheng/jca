We love to gather

We get together Sundays, Fridays, Mondays to Thursdays. Pretty much everyday!

It’s true—there’s always some kind of meeting going on at Journey not because we love meetings, but because we love doing life together. Whether that means breaking bread, worshipping or serving, we know it’s better together. Check out the following corporate gathering times to get plugged into the community. Everyone is welcome.

Sundays

One of the best ways to get to know our community is to visit us on a Sunday. Our Sunday celebrations are prayerful, Spirit-filled, Bible-based times of worship, teaching and community building. We would love to see you this Sunday. You can find us at the following times and locations:
