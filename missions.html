Disciples of
all nations

Jesus called His believers to a great and glorious mission:
"Go and make disciples of all nations." Matthew 28:19

God has called us to missions both locally, in our neighborhoods and in our country, and globally, “to the end of the earth.” Therefore, we highly value our missional partners and see them as essential components of the church.

We’ve teamed up with various ministries and nonprofit organizations in the Atlanta area, and we hope to continue to partner with them to reach out to the poor and underprivileged. Journey Church is also committed to sending teams to locations all around the world on short-term (weeks to months) and long-term (one year or more) trips. Let’s be strong and courageous and reach cities and the world for Jesus Christ.

Local Partners

"But seek the welfare of the city where I have sent you into exile, and pray to the Lord on its behalf, for in its welfare you will find your welfare." Jeremiah 29:7

City of Refuge

City of Refuge exists to bring light, hope, and transformation to individuals and families of Atlanta. We partner with people in crisis to help break the cycle of poverty through services in health and wellness, housing, human trafficking intervention, vocational training and youth development.


City Hope Community

City Hope Community’s mission is to mobilize and unite individuals and churches in taking up the care and cause of the socially vulnerable. We long to see communities where members can flourish and extend God’s love as salt and light to the world.


Human Coalition

Human Coalition is a champion of life, committed to an audacious mission: to transform our culture of death into a culture of life—to end abortion in America.
