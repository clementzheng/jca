Giving

20% of every dollar goes to missions

At Journey, we’re committed to maturing, multiplying and mobilizing worshipers of Jesus Christ in Atlanta and all over the world. We invite you to partner with us in achieving this vision. Thank you for supporting this community through financial giving.

There are several ways you can give:

By Mail

The Journey Church of Atlanta
PO Box 15575
Atlanta, GA 30333

In Person

During our regular Sunday offering

Online

Via PayPal to giving@jcatlanta.org*

*If you regularly give online and would like to save JCA the Paypal fees deducted from each gift, you can do so from the PayPal mobile app or by going directly to the Paypal website (www.paypal.com). Click "Send Money," enter desired amount, select the "Friends and Family" option, and then "Continue." Enter "giving@jcatlanta.org" as the recipient and click “Continue” to complete the process. If you need any assistance, please contact Claudia Robbie or email admin@jcatlanta.org.
