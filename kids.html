Little Travelers

Let the children come to me

Do not hinder them, for to such belongs the kingdom of God. Mark 10:14

During our 1:30 PM “Eastside” Sunday service, all children in grades kindergarten and above are invited to join our Sunday School program, “Little Travelers.” We strive to teach children about the love of God and how to become more like Jesus Christ. Every week, children learn about stories from the Bible, and summaries of each lesson are emailed to parents so you can more fully get involved.

If you have children younger than kindergarten age, please let us know via the form below so we can prepare to welcome your child.

How to Participate

1:30 PM at “Eastside”

Westminster Presbyterian Church
1438 Sheridan Road NE
Atlanta, GA 30324

Before your Sunday visit, please fill out the form below to let us know the names and ages of your children, as well as any allergies or other important notes.

As you enter the building, talk to any greeter wearing a blue “Welcoming” nametag and they will happily escort your family to the “Little Travelers” room.

After worship service, come back to the same “Little Travelers” room to sign your children out. You’ll receive an email from our teachers with a lesson summary and, occasionally, weekday activities to help your children be engaged from Sunday to Sunday.
